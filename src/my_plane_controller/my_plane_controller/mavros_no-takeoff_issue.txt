✔ Why 22 in middle worked

🔥 Final correct understanding

ArduPilot Plane uses a state machine, not simple waypoint execution.

Command	Meaning
16	Fly to waypoint (requires airborne state)
22	Transition from ground → air (takeoff state trigger)
21	Transition from air → ground (landing)
✅ Final working solution

Your working mission became:

Why this works:
16 → establishes mission entry safely
22 → triggers takeoff state machine
remaining 16 → normal navigation in air
